# Privacy Policy — Prompt Enhancer

**Last updated:** June 9, 2026

Prompt Enhancer is a Chrome Extension that rewrites rough user prompts into high-quality structured prompts using AI. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

---

## What We Collect

Prompt Enhancer stores the following data **locally on your device only** using `chrome.storage.local`:

- **API Key** — If you choose to provide your own OpenRouter, OpenAI, or Gemini API key, it is stored locally and never sent to any server we control.
- **Prompt History** — The last 10 prompts you have enhanced, including both the original and enhanced versions, are stored locally for your convenience.

## What We Don't Collect

- We do **not** collect personal information.
- We do **not** collect browsing history or usage analytics.
- We do **not** run any tracking, telemetry, or analytics code.
- We do **not** have user accounts or registration.
- We do **not** operate any servers that receive your data.

## Third-Party Services

When you enhance a prompt, the extension sends your text to **OpenRouter** (https://openrouter.ai) for processing. OpenRouter's API is used to route your prompt to an AI model that rewrites it.

- If you provide your own API key, the request is made directly from your browser to OpenRouter using your key.
- If you do not provide a key, the extension uses a built-in key to make the request on your behalf.

Please refer to [OpenRouter's Privacy Policy](https://openrouter.ai/privacy) for details on how they handle data.

## Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to us or stored on external servers under our control. Your prompt history and API key remain on your machine until you:

- Clear the extension data, or
- Uninstall the extension, or
- Manually delete your API key and history through the extension settings.

## How to Delete Your Data

1. Click the Prompt Enhancer icon in your Chrome toolbar.
2. Go to **Settings** (options page).
3. Remove your API key from the input field and click **Save**.
4. To clear prompt history: open the overlay on any page, click **History**, then click **Clear History**.
5. To fully remove all data, uninstall the extension from `chrome://extensions`.

## Children's Privacy

Prompt Enhancer is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the extension package. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
